Privacy Policy

Your information. Handled with care.

This policy explains how Konwaba High School collects, uses and protects personal information when you interact with our website, admissions process, and communications.

Effective
21 Feb 2026
Scope
Website + Admissions
Contact

1. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Enquiry details (name, email, phone number, message)
  • Admissions information (learner and parent/guardian details)
  • Communication preferences and consent choices

We may also collect limited technical information to run the website, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and basic analytics.

2. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide requested information
  • Process admissions applications and related administration
  • Communicate about events, notices, and school updates (where you opted in)
  • Maintain website security and performance

3. Cookies and analytics

Cookies may be used for essential website functionality and basic analytics. You can usually control cookies via your browser settings. If we introduce additional tracking, we will update this policy accordingly.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website or school processes, under appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

5. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. No website can guarantee complete security, but we continuously improve our controls.

6. Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law and school governance practices.

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal and operational requirements. Contact us using the details below.
