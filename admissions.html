Important Admission Notes

Please note that primary vacancies are available for Grade 8 learners, with strictly limited placement opportunities in Grades 9 and 10. We recommend early application to secure a position.

Medium of Instruction

Konwaba High School is an English-medium institution. Proficiency in English is a prerequisite for admission as all core subjects are taught in English.

Submission Policy: ALL applications must be hand-delivered to the Admissions Office at 16 Cawood Street, Cradock.

Documentation: Applications will ONLY be accepted if ALL required certified documentation (as per the application checklist) is submitted.

International Students: Applicants from outside South Africa must possess a valid passport and study permit before an application can be considered.
