Medium of Instruction & Policy

Konwaba High School is an English-medium school (Grades 8-12). Proficiency in English is a prerequisite for admission as all core subjects are taught in English.

Vacancies are primarily available for Grade 8, with limited opportunities in Grades 9 and 10. We strongly recommend applying to more than one institution.

Documentation

Applications are only accepted if all required certified documentation is submitted in full.

International Applicants

Non-South African applicants must provide a valid passport and study permit prior to consideration.
