Who We Are

Located in Nxuba (Cradock), Konwaba High School is a premier learning community where heritage meets modern industry. We bridge the gap between academic theory and master-level practical skills, ensuring every learner is equipped for the real world.

To provide world-class education where students learn accountability, fulfill their potential, and reach their dreams.

To offer top-notch education in a conducive environment, preparing students for success in academics and life beyond.
